All incoming freshmen who do not live within 30 miles from campus are required to live in the residence halls for one academic year. This is a university requirement and takes effect once you accept RIT's offer of admission.

The Application Process

  1. Complete the RIT housing contract online and pick a roommate.

    You can click here to fill out the contract and choose your roommate.

  2. Get an appointment time to pick your room.

    You will get an email with your appointment time for when you can access the myHousing website to select your own room in the residence halls.

  3. Select your room online.

    Your appointment time starts and end in late May. This is the earliest you can go and select a room. You can select a room even if you don't have a roommate. Appointment times are assigned based on when you pay your tuition deposit.
