Meal Plans

Since most freshman students are required to live on campus, they need to have a meal plan to get food from the dining areas. There are three different meal plans available depending on how often the student eats.

Tiger 20

  • Cost Per Semester: $2,950
  • For students who eat 3 meals a day, 7 days a week
  • Spend nearly $20 a week in Dining Dollars

Tiger 14

  • Cost Per Semester: $2,831
  • For students who eat 2 meals a day, 7 days a week
  • Spend nearly $33 a week in Dining Dollars

Tiger 10

  • Cost Per Semester: $2,724
  • For students who eat 1-2 meals a day, 7 days a week
  • Spend nearly $45 a week in Dining Dollars

Dining Dollars can be used at any RIT dining location, vending machines, or convenience stores. The great thing about it is that you avoid the 8% sales tax. You can receive a 10% discount if used at Gracie's. Dining Dollars rolls from Fall Semester into Spring Semester but expires at the end of that semester.
