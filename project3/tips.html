Here are some tips provided by fellow college students. If you take the advice, you will find college less stressful than it already is.

Tips*

  • Always go to class. This is SO important because you will fall behind.
  • Get involved. All the knowledge in the world won’t help you if you come out of college with no experience or professional relationships.
  • ALWAYS back up your files.
  • Never leave your clothes in a washer after the cycle is over. Not doing this is rude, and people WILL pull your clothes out and set them somewhere.
  • Get to know your professors. College is just as much about networking as it is about sitting in class.
  • Try out as many clubs as you can. Feel no obligation to them if you don’t like them. College is about finding out what you love to do.
  • Make sure you are organized. Get a calendar and write down due dates and reminders.
  • If you have a question, don't be afraid to go to your professor or TA's office hours or send them an email.
  • Make sure you sign up for next semester classes early. You don't want to be stuck on a waitlist.
  • Take advantage of many of the services and activities offered - like tutoring, the fitness center, health services, and clubs or sports.

*Adapted from CollegeInfoGeek

The Strong National Museum of Play

1 Manhattan Square Dr, Rochester, NY 14607

One of the largest museums in the United States, The Strong is an interactive, collections-based museum devoted to the history and exploration of play.

Rochester Science Museum and Planetarium

657 East Ave, Rochester, NY 14607

Follow your curiosity from the depths of the oceans to the edge of the universe. Push the limits of your imagination to create never-before-seen inventions.

Erie Canal Trail

The Erie Canal Trail is great for joggers or cyclists. It is one of the world's most famous man-made waterways and spans between Albany and Buffalo.

Java's Cafe

16 Gibbs St, Rochester, NY 14604

Java's Cafe is a study spot with a hip vibe and a menu of pastries and coffee.

Lux Lounge

666 South Ave, Rochester, NY 14620

A bar that has a backyard and firepit, a pool table, jukebox and many drinks.

The Marketplace Mall

1 Miracle Mile Dr, Rochester, NY 14623

A shopping center that has more than 110 stores and a food court. You can get all your shopping done here.
